【Chinese】
可视化结果主要包括三部分内容：
图1展示的是2010年中国各地区人均预期寿命情况，通过左下角的滑块可以筛选特定范围的省份
图2展示的是1990-2010年对应地区人均预期寿命变化情况（按照男性、女性和整体三种情况分类），可点击图例进行筛选
图3展示的2010年对应地区卫生支出占GDP百分比的情况，可点击图例进行筛选
这是一个可交互的视图，点击图1中的不同地区，图2和图3会自动显示对应地区的情况
链接地址：https://lifecong.github.io/visualization/Chinese.html


【English】
Three parts results are included in this visualization project.
Figure 1 shows the life expectancy of different provinces of China in 2010. The slider in the lower left corner can be used to filter showing provinces based on the range of life expectancy.
Figure 2 shows the trend of life expectancy (by sex) from 1990 to 2010. The legend could be clicked to show different situations
Figure 3 shows the proportion of health expenditure to GDP in the corresponding provinces in 2010. More information could be shown when moving the mouse to the pie.
This is an interactive view. Click the different province in Figure 1. Figures 2 and 3 will automatically display the data of corresponding areas.
Link address: https://lifecong.github.io/visualization/English.html
